osu! relax tournaments

Take a cup of coffee and relax ☕... The osu tournaments are back!
This series of osu! tournaments specializes in using the relax mod, players will compete against each other in a fun mappool with cool song choices that covers many skillsets. This tournament lasts one day (meaning that everything is run in 6~ hours during one sitting) and the win condition is set to accuracy.

Join the discord!

In the discord you will find a bunch of other participants who are actively looking for people to chat with! Find your best teammates in there or simply get inside so that you don't miss the latest announcements.

In order to complete the sign-up process, you must be inside the discord server! Participants will have to post their match results on every tournament round in a separate discord channel, our staff will be monitoring the channel to ensure the results are correct and that the tournament brackets are updated.

Information

Tournament format

  • The tournament lasts one day.
  • The win condition is accuracy.
  • The game format is 1v1 single elimination.
  • The tournament rank range is from #3000 to unlimited.
  • The mappool star rate is from about 7⭐.
  • This tournament uses the relax mod.
  • There is no player limit for the tournament.
  • The matches are to the best of 7 (first to get to 4 wins)
  • The grand finals match is to the best of 9.
  • You must be inside the discord server to play.

Match procedures

  • 1. Someone creates a multiplayer lobby and invites their opponent.
  • 2. Wait for your opponent to join the lobby. Remember to save the multiplayer link at the beginning of the chat.
  • 3. Name the multiplayer room: GCT (Player1) vs (Player2)
  • 4. When both players are ready, type !roll in chat. The highest roll is the roll winner.
  • 5. The winner of the roll bans the first and the third map, they also get to pick the first map after the bans.
  • 6. The loser of the roll bans the second and the fourth map. Remember that bans must be done in order! (1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4)
  • 7. The tiebreaker is played when the match score is 3-3.
  • 8. The winner of the set grabs the match results and posts them on discord to advance to the next round.

FAQ

Your rank must be below #3000 to be elegible to participate (e.g. if your rank is #200.000, you're able to register), to sign-up you have to comment in the tournament forum post your current rank, then join the discord and change your nickname to your osu! username (you can revert it back after the verification process is done). You should get the participant role in a few days after you signed up, if you did NOT get the discord role before the tournament starts please ping Didah#9887 urgently because bracket pairings are done via the list of participants with the role in discord.

If the tournament hasn't started yet, message Didah#9887 and tell him that you're not interested in playing the tournament anymore, your participant role will be removed from discord and you won't be added to the tournament brackets. If the tournament has started and the brackets are announced, do not enter the multiplayer game and tell your opponent that you will not be playing the tournament.

Give your opponents a reasonable amount of time to join the multiplayer lobby (there's been multiple cases in the past where participants may have fallen asleep, had network connectivity problems or simply had other more important tasks to do). If your opponent still doesn't show up after pinging them multiple times on discord and you have waited an estimated amount of 15~ minutes, go to the #results channel in discord and include a message with the provided results format saying that your opponent didn't show up.

You should be constantly looking at the tournament brackets in challonge to know who you're playing against, when you get paired with an opponent, feel free to invite them to the mupltiplayer lobby (this means you don't have to wait for specific round times to play against someone, instead the round will start as soon as both players from the bracket pairing are prepared). Players are allowed to take 10 minute breaks when their match has finished if they need so, in that case, the opponent will have to wait until the other player is ready (if the break time gets excessively delayed, the player may get removed from the tournament brackets).

In case someone quits the lobby in-game, the player will lose the map and will be able to re-join the lobby. In uncommon cases where there are gameplay issues at the very beginning of the map (the first few seconds) such as tablet disconnectivity, very high latency due to having the storyboard enabled or similar related issues, the map may be played again from the beginning IF the other player agrees with it.
